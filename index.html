Story

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was born in Rameswaram in 1931. He rose from a humble background to become a top scientist and "Missile Man" of India. Kalam served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007. He inspired millions with his simplicity, vision, and love for education.
